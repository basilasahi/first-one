The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for
about an hour now. On her desk, lying between
the copies of Nature, New Scientist,
and all the other scientific journals her work had
appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of
On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite
book since college, and the longer she spent
in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spend last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster
with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaming that Work is the refuge of
people who have nothing beter to do
. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the
secrets of the llama DNA
, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had
something beter to do.
